Abducktion is a head-tilting casual game where cultists and aliens must work together to _abduck_ all the ducks of the earth. Help the cultists by tilting their world using your device to align them with the aliens' duck abduction beam technology.

This game was made as an entry for [Global Game Jam 2016](https://globalgamejam.org/) with the theme: **Ritual** and was made in two days.

## Responsibilities

- Programmed the whole game
- Designed the game with [Kaizan Caballes](https://twitter.com/kaizancaballes)

## What's cool about it?

- Utilized device accelorometer as player's input
- Beautiful art by [Jeremy Plana](https://www.instagram.com/jeremissing/) and [Renzo Hipolito](https://www.instagram.com/gaijin_shoukoushi/)
